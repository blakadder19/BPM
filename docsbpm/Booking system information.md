**Descriptions**

### **Beginners 1**

* ### No experience needed

* ### 4-week term 

* ### New students welcome in Week 1 & Week 2

This is your introduction to rhythm, connection, and confidence.

### **Beginners 2**

* Automatic progression from Beginners 1  
* 4-week term  
* New students join in Week 1

This level focuses on mastering the fundamentals, including basic steps and weight transfer.

The goal is to build a strong foundation and confidence in the basics before progressing to more advanced material.

We recommend students stay in Beginners 2 for up to 6 months if possible, but progression is ultimately decided by the teachers based on each student’s development.

\*Students may be moved to different levels, to suit their current requirements, at our instructor's discretion

**Improvers**

For dancers who’ve completed Beginners 1 & 2 (or have mastered the basic)

* 8 week terms  
* 3 terms \= 1 full course  
* Each term is different; we don't repeat content

You can stay in Improvers for up to a year, growing stronger each term. After completing one full course, you may attend Intermediate alongside Improvers.

Our teachers may guide you to the level that helps you grow best.

We focus on quality of movement, not just memorising patterns.

\*Students may be moved to different levels, to suit their current requirements, at our instructor's discretion

### **Intermediate**

For dancers who've completed at least one full improvers course (or have 8+ months experience, including beginners)

TEACHER APPROVAL REQUIRED

* 8-week terms  
* 3 terms \= 1 full course  
* New content every term

Dancers often stay in Intermediate up to 2 years, developing confidence, control, and style.

Teachers may recommend level adjustments to support your progress and growth.

\*Students may be moved to different levels, to suit their current requirements, at our instructor's discretion

### **Technique and Advanced**

Advanced entry includes an assessment by a core instructor, based on:

* Skill level

* Mindset & willingness to learn

* Personal dance goals (social dancing, performing, competing etc.)

**Structure & Focus**  
At this level, we focus on refining technique, precision, musicality, and personal expression.

Students may trial Advanced. If we feel more preparation is needed, we may recommend:

* Repeating Intermediate

* Adding Technique classes

* Combining levels

This is designed to support long-term growth, build confidence, and avoid frustration.

Growth \> rushing

### **Assessments (All Levels)**

Each level includes a simple assessment checklist used by instructors as a guide.

This helps:

* Ensure consistency in teaching  
* Give students clear feedback  
* Highlight what needs improvement before moving to the next level

This allows students to clearly understand their progress and what they need to work on before advancing.

\*Students may be moved to different levels, to suit their current requirements, at our instructor's discretion

**BPM Behaviour Policy & Code of Conduct**

					

**Purpose and Scope**

This Policy ensures a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all BPM community members. It applies to all staff, instructors, assistants, volunteers, students, contractors, guests, and participants, both in-person and online.

					

**Zero Tolerance Statement**					

BPM maintains a zero-tolerance approach to harassment, discrimination, intimidation, bullying, abuse, or inappropriate conduct. Any breach may result in immediate disciplinary action, including removal from classes, termination of agreements, or permanent exclusion without refund.

					

**Expected Standards of Conduct**

All individuals are expected to act professionally, respect boundaries and consent, follow instructor direction, communicate appropriately, and contribute positively to a safe learning environment.

					

**Prohibited Behaviour**

Prohibited behaviour includes, but is not limited to: verbal or physical abuse, intimidation, discriminatory language, non-consensual contact, disruptive conduct, and online harassment or defamatory behaviour.

					

**Reporting and Enforcement**

Concerns should be reported promptly to BPM management. All matters will be handled confidentially and without retaliation. BPM reserves the right to investigate and take appropriate action based on the severity of the conduct.

					

**No Refunds**

Where access to classes, events, or services is revoked due to misconduct, no refunds or credits will be issued.

					

**Acknowledgement**

Participation in any BPM activity constitutes acknowledgement of, and agreement to comply with, this Policy.

**Student Liability & Responsibility Policy**		

				

**1\. Purpose**					

This policy outlines the responsibilities of students, members, and attendees of BPM Dance Studio in relation to the care, use, and respect of Studio property, facilities, equipment, and the property of others.

					

**2\. Scope**

This policy applies to all individuals attending BPM Dance Studio, including students, members, drop-in attendees, workshop and event participants, and visitors at BPM-operated activities.

					

**3\. Responsibility for Studio Property**

**Duty of Care**

All attendees are expected to treat the Studio premises, furnishings, fixtures, and equipment with care, use equipment only for its intended purpose, and report accidental damage immediately.

					

**4\. Damage, Vandalism & Misuse**

Where damage is caused by deliberate action, reckless behaviour, negligence, or failure to follow Studio rules, the responsible individual may be held financially liable for reasonable repair, replacement, or cleaning costs.

					

**5\. Theft & Removal of Property**

The removal of Studio property without permission or interference with the personal belongings of others may result in investigation, suspension of access, or referral to law enforcement where appropriate.

					

**6\. Disciplinary Action**

Depending on severity, BPM Dance Studio may require reimbursement, issue warnings, suspend or terminate access or membership without refund, exclude individuals from future activities, or report matters to relevant authorities.

					

**7\. Minors**

Where the responsible individual is under 18, parents or legal guardians may be held responsible for damage or loss to the extent permitted by law.

					

**8\. Appeals & Disputes**

Individuals may submit a written explanation or dispute. BPM Dance Studio will review all relevant information before making a final decision.

					

**9\. Relationship to Other Policies**

This policy should be read in conjunction with the BPM Behaviour Policy & Code of Conduct, Terms & Conditions, and Health & Safety Policy.

				

**10\. Policy Review**

BPM Dance Studio reserves the right to review and update this policy. Any material changes will be communicated in advance.

**1\. Scope & Applicability**

These Terms & Conditions (“T\&Cs;”) apply to all companies, artists, schools, organisations, and individuals (“the Hirer”) renting or using BPM’s studio and community space (“the Studio”). A booking request does not constitute confirmation. Confirmation is made only once BPM confirms availability in writing and issues an invoice. Availability is held for 24 hours only. Failure to pay the required deposit within this time releases the slot.

					

**3\. Payment Terms**  
**Classes & Rehearsals:** 50% deposit upon confirmation; remaining 50% due no later than 7 days prior. **Events:** 50% deposit upon confirmation; remaining 50% due no later than 2 months prior or at booking, whichever comes first.

					

**4\. Rates & Timekeeping**

Rates are charged per hour. A 5-minute leeway is allowed for classes and rehearsals. Delays beyond this may be charged at the hourly rate at management’s discretion.

					

**5\. Cancellation Policy**  
**Classes & Rehearsals:**

**Events:**

2 months prior: 50% | 1 month prior: 80% | Final month / same day: 100%

**7\. Noise Restrictions**

**8\. Prohibited Activities**

**9\. Liability, Damage & Theft**

Hirers are fully responsible for their attendees. BPM accepts no liability for loss or damage to personal property. Any damage, theft, or vandalism will be charged in full to the Hirer.

					

**10\. Insurance**

Failure to comply may result in fines, termination of booking, or refusal of future bookings. All decisions are at BPM management’s discretion.
